About Me

|

I’m Ashwith Frank, a developer who enjoys building practical and visually clean digital experiences. I focus on creating things that are simple to use, easy to understand, and purposeful. Whether it’s a website, an Android app, or a small game, I like working on ideas that actually feel useful and polished.


I’m constantly learning and experimenting with new concepts while keeping my work lightweight and efficient. I believe good design doesn’t need to be complicated - it just needs clarity, consistency, and attention to detail. This portfolio is a small reflection of how I approach my work.

What I Do

Web Development

I work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build responsive layouts with smooth animations and clean structure. I enjoy designing interfaces that look modern while staying readable and user-friendly across devices. Glassy UI, subtle motion, and balanced spacing are things I care about.

Android Development

I build native apps with a focus on simple logic, performance, and usability. I prefer writing clean, understandable code and designing interfaces that feel natural to use. My goal is always to make apps that are reliable, lightweight, and easy to interact with.
