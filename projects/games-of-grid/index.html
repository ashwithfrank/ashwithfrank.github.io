Overview

Games of Grid is a sliding tile puzzle game available on the Google Play Store. The goal is simple: arrange numbered tiles in ascending order by sliding them into an empty space. Sounds easy — but planning ahead is everything.

The game offers two grid sizes (3x3 and 4x4) and two difficulty modes. Easy mode allows movement in all 8 directions including diagonals, while Hard mode restricts you to 4 directions only, making every move matter more. It was published under AlphaOmega Studio and has had 10+ downloads on the Play Store.

What I Learned

Publishing a game on the Play Store was a completely different experience from just building it. I had to navigate the developer console, set up store listings, handle content ratings, write a privacy policy, and optimize the APK for different Android versions. Getting through the review process and seeing it go live was a big milestone.

On the game side, working on a tile puzzle taught me to think carefully about state management — tracking which tiles are where, validating moves, and detecting when the puzzle is solved efficiently.
